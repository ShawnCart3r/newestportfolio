About Me

Hi, my name is Shawn Carter, a web developer with a knack for front-end development and a love for bringing ideas to life. I've always been drawn to building random things and figuring out how they work, so diving into web development felt like a natural fit.

When I’m not coding, you’ll usually find me working on models, battling it out in Magic: The Gathering, or writing as a creative outlet. These hobbies keep me inspired and help me bring fresh perspectives to my work.

I’m passionate about crafting user-friendly, visually engaging experiences and enjoy the challenge of turning ideas into something functional and fun. Whether it’s on the web or at the gaming table, I’m always up for creating something awesome.
