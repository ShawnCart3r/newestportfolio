About Me

Hi, my name is Shawn Carter — a web developer with a strong focus on front-end development and a passion for turning ideas into intuitive, engaging digital experiences. My curiosity for how things work naturally led me to coding, where I thrive on building thoughtful, functional solutions from the ground up.

Outside of development, I stay creatively energized through hands-on hobbies like working on models, writing, and playing strategy games like Magic: The Gathering. These interests sharpen my attention to detail, problem-solving, and storytelling — skills I bring into every project I work on.

I’m driven by the challenge of crafting user-friendly, visually engaging experiences that balance form and function. Whether collaborating with a team or coding solo, I’m always looking to build something meaningful — on the web or at the gaming table.
